## Overview

The Competency Management System helps organizations:

- Define and track employee competencies
- Conduct periodic assessments
- Identify skill gaps
- Create personalized development plans

### Key Features

- **Self & Leader Assessment**: Structured evaluation process
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify areas for improvement
- **Individual Development Plans (IDP)**: Track your growth journey
- **Visual Reports**: Radar charts and progress tracking

## For Employees

### 1. Self-Assessment

**When**: During an active assessment cycle

**Steps**:

1. Click **My Assessment** in the navigation menu
2. Review the competencies required for your role and career band
3. For each competency:
   - Read the behavioral indicators for each level (1-5)
   - Select your current level honestly
   - Add notes to explain your rating (optional)
4. Click **Submit Assessment** when complete

**Tips**:

- Be honest and objective
- Use specific examples in your notes
- Review behavioral indicators carefully
- Save your progress regularly

### 2. Viewing Assessment Results

**When**: After your leader finalizes the assessment

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to **My Assessment**
2. Click **View Results** on a completed assessment
3. Review:
   - **Summary Cards**: Your scores and gaps
   - **Strengths**: Competencies where you meet/exceed requirements
   - **Areas for Improvement**: Competencies below required level

**Understanding the Gap**:

- **Positive Gap (+)**: You exceed requirements ✅
- **Zero Gap (0)**: You meet requirements ✅
- **Negative Gap (-)**: Development needed ⚠️

### 3. Creating an Individual Development Plan (IDP)

**When**: After reviewing your assessment results

**Steps**:

1. From the Assessment Results page, click **Create Development Plan**
2. Fill in your development goal (e.g., "Prepare for Senior Developer role")
3. Set start and end dates (typically 3-6 months)
4. Add development activities:
   - Select the competency to improve
   - Choose activity type:
     - **Training**: Formal courses or workshops
     - **Mentoring**: Learning from experienced colleagues
     - **Project Challenge**: Hands-on project work
     - **Self Study**: Books, online courses, research
   - Describe what you'll do
   - Set a target date (optional)
5. Click **Create IDP**

**Best Practices**:

- Focus on 2-3 key competencies at a time
- Make activities specific and measurable
- Set realistic timelines
- Discuss your plan with your leader

### 4. Tracking Your IDP Progress

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to **My IDP**
2. View your current plan and progress percentage
3. Mark activities as complete by clicking the checkmark button
4. Monitor due dates (overdue items are highlighted in red)

**Tips**:

- Update your progress weekly
- Add evidence/notes when marking activities complete
- Discuss progress with your leader in 1-on-1s

## For Leaders

### 1. Assessing Team Members

**When**: After team members complete self-assessment

**Steps**:

1. Navigate to **Team Assessments** (coming soon)
2. Select a team member with status "Pending Leader Assessment"
3. Review their self-assessment scores and notes
4. For each competency:
   - Consider their self-rating
   - Add your leader rating (1-5)
   - Provide constructive feedback in notes
5. Click **Submit Leader Assessment**

**Best Practices**:

- Use specific examples from recent work
- Be fair and consistent across team members
- Provide actionable feedback
- Schedule a discussion meeting

### 2. Finalizing Assessments

**When**: After discussion with team member

**Steps**:

1. Meet with the team member to discuss ratings
2. Agree on final scores together
3. In the system, enter the agreed final scores
4. Add overall feedback
5. Click **Finalize Assessment**

**This triggers**:

- Gap analysis calculation
- Assessment results become visible to employee
- Employee can now create their IDP

### 3. Supporting IDP Development

**Responsibilities**:

- Review and approve team members' IDPs
- Provide resources and opportunities
- Monitor progress in 1-on-1 meetings
- Adjust plans as needed

## For HR/Admin

### 1. Managing Competency Framework

#### Creating Competency Groups

1. Navigate to **Admin** → **Competencies**
2. Click **Create Group**
3. Enter group name (e.g., "Technical Skills", "Leadership")
4. Click **Save**

#### Adding Competencies

1. Select a competency group
2. Click **Add Competency**
3. Enter:
   - Competency name
   - Description
   - Behavioral indicators for levels 1-5
4. Click **Save**

**Tips**:

- Use clear, observable behaviors
- Make levels progressive (1 = basic, 5 = expert)
- Align with industry standards

### 2. Setting Competency Requirements

1. Navigate to **Admin** → **Competencies** → **Requirements**
2. View the matrix: Competencies (rows) × Career Bands (columns)
3. For each cell, enter the required level (1-5)
4. Click **Save Changes**

**Guidelines**:

- Junior roles: Levels 1-2
- Mid-level roles: Levels 2-3
- Senior roles: Levels 3-4
- Expert roles: Levels 4-5

### 3. Managing Assessment Cycles

#### Creating a Cycle

1. Navigate to **Admin** → **Competencies** → **Cycles**
2. Click **Create Cycle**
3. Enter:
   - Cycle name (e.g., "Q1 2026 Assessment")
   - Start date
   - End date
4. Click **Create**

**Important**:

- Only one cycle can be ACTIVE at a time
- Cycles cannot overlap
- Employees can only assess during active cycles

#### Assigning Users to Cycle

Once a cycle is **ACTIVE**, you can bulk assign assessments to all eligible employees (those with an assigned Career Band):

1. In the **Assessment Cycles** list, find the active cycle.
2. Click the **Actions** menu (three dots).
3. Select **Assign Users**.
4. Confirm the action.
   - The system will create assessment records for all eligible users who don't already have one in this cycle.

### 4. Monitoring Progress

1. **DRAFT**: Cycle created but not started
2. **ACTIVE**: Employees can self-assess
3. **COMPLETED**: Cycle ended, no more assessments

## FAQ

### General

**Q: How often are assessments conducted?**
A: Typically quarterly or bi-annually, as determined by HR.

**Q: Can I update my assessment after submitting?**
A: No, once submitted you cannot edit. Contact your leader if you need changes.

**Q: What if I disagree with my leader's rating?**
A: Discuss during the finalization meeting. The final score should be mutually agreed upon.

### Self-Assessment

**Q: Should I rate myself based on current performance or potential?**
A: Rate your current, demonstrated performance with evidence.

**Q: What if I don't have experience with a competency?**
A: Rate yourself as Level 1 and note that you're developing this skill.

### IDP

**Q: Can I have multiple IDPs?**
A: Only one active IDP at a time. Complete or cancel the current one before creating a new one.

**Q: What if I can't complete an activity by the target date?**
A: Discuss with your leader. You can adjust timelines as needed.

**Q: Can I add activities to an existing IDP?**
A: Currently no. Plan your activities carefully when creating the IDP.

### Technical

**Q: The system is slow/not loading. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact IT support.

**Q: I accidentally deleted something. Can it be recovered?**
A: Contact your HR admin immediately. Some data may be recoverable.
